International Business

Our international relationships in the meat industry have allowed us to distribute our products across North America. We export and import products from the United States, Canada, and Mexico. About Us

We are YUSA Traders Inc. We were founded in 2007 and have lead in the distribution and supply of various meat products in North America. Our partnership with North South Foods Group Inc. has allowed us to have an international reach. We truly believe in delivering high quality products efficiently to all our customers. High quality products and quick transportation come from collaboration and open communication between all of our suppliers and distributors.
